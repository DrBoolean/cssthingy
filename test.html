FOX

The Fox Theater is located at 241 South Main Street in Old Town Salinas. It first opened in November 1921 and was used for movie attractions for many years. In 2004 The Papering Theater rented out the Fox as a venue for their theatrical productions. It was there that Amy and Julian met and fell in love while working on the production of ‘The Rocky Horror Show.’ In 2007, the theater was purchased by Anthony Lane and renovated to its present state. The theater boasts a stage, dance floor, balcony with theater seats and two Art Deco bars. Amy and Julian’s wedding date is 11 years to the date that they started dating and they are excited to be getting hitched at the place where it all began. View our Save the Date video here.
